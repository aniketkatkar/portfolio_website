A little about me

I'm a Freelance Designer and a Developer Hybrid. But I hate conversations about what "stack" I'm using. I love programming because it is a means to an end - creating a application that customers love. I'm not the kind of tech guy who experiments with new stuff for the sake of it. I view technology through the lens of designers for customers benefits - can a piece of technology improve customer experience somehow (e.g. make the app run faster, create beautiful interfaces)? If so, great - I'll check it out. Turning ideas into beautiful Android applications and Websites is what I do.
